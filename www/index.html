Read this if you live in Massachusetts/New England and want to help with the Sandy relief efforts, but feel that you are too far away, or don't know how.

Follow @FriendsofNYC

Beginning on Monday, November 5th, donations for much-needed goods will be accepted at the loading dock at Huntington Home Systems, in Newton, Massachusetts. Trucks will be sent to the boroughs of New York that are in desperate need of food, clothing, and other day-to-day essentials that we have all grown to take for granted.

WHAT YOU CAN DONATE

  • New and gently used clothing for men, women and children
  • Coats, hats, gloves
  • Pillows and blankets
  • Non-perishable food items
  • Shower/bath products - toothpaste, toothbrushes and feminine products
  • Batteries, flashlights and other small household items
  • Toys for kids
  • Cleaning supplies - everything from mops and buckets to cleaning products and towels
  • Dust masks
  • Diapers for children (and the elderly)
  • Toilet paper and paper towels
  • Baby wipes and baby formula

WHERE AND WHEN?

Huntington Home Systems
  • 369 Boylston Street (Rt. 9) -- Newton, MA 02459
  • Beginning Monday November 5th at 9:30 am -- daily from 9:30am until 6pm
